Welcome to remsgr

remsgr is a recreation of the MSN Messenger/Windows Live Messenger servers, not affiliated with Microsoft, for you to enjoy the program once again.

The server is currently not public, however will eventually be after alpha & beta testing, join our Discord if you wanna have instant news about remsgr!
